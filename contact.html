FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

01. Where are you Based?

We are based in Silverton Pretoria, however we offer our services to most of Southern Africa.

02. What Services Do you Offer?

From designing and manufacturing unique furniture to creating stunning decor, we have you covered. Our services extend to brand activations, business conferences, and music celebrations/concerts. Let us bring your vision to life and make your event one to remember.

03. How do you price?

At Langa Events, we understand that every event is unique and special. That's why we tailor our pricing to fit your specific needs and budget. Our quotes are based on your requirements to ensure that we can execute your event successfully.

04. What is the Process to Book you for an Event?

Get In Touch with us !!

Getting in touch with us is easy - just send us a message on WhatsApp at 076 721 8716 or shoot us an email at info@langaevents.com. Once we understand your needs, we will put together a personalized quote just for you.

If you like what you see, we will schedule a meeting with our talented team to dive deeper into your vision and figure out the best way to make your event shine. So why wait? Let's start planning your dream event together today!

05. How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for smaller events, and 2-3 months for larger events like weddings and corporate galas. This allows us ample time to plan, design, and execute your vision to perfection. However, we understand that sometimes events come up unexpectedly - feel free to reach out and we'll do our best to accommodate your timeline.

06. Do you provide setup and breakdown services?

Yes! Our full-service packages include complete setup before your event and breakdown after it ends. Our experienced team handles all the heavy lifting so you can focus on enjoying your special day. We ensure everything is perfectly arranged and leave your venue exactly as we found it.
